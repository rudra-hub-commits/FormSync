Auto-Suggest Discrepancy Notes

🧠 AI
Smart Form Validation Engine
Cross-check form combinations for known conflicts (e.g. two mutually exclusive exclusions on the same policy). Flag illogical pairings before binding — catches underwriting errors no one else catches.
🔄 Workflow
Policy Version Diff
Upload two policy PDFs (renewal vs prior year) and instantly see which forms were added, removed, or changed — a full line-by-line endorsement diff that takes seconds instead of 30 minutes.
📊 Data
Required Forms Rule Engine
Define rules per state and policy type: "HO3 in Massachusetts must always include HO 01 20." Automatically alerts if a mandatory form is missing from the policy. Eliminates E&O exposure.
⚡ Speed
Batch Policy Processing
Upload 10 policy PDFs at once against a single PUMAA page. Get a summary table showing all missing and extra forms across all policies — one click to review the whole book.
🔗 Share
One-Click Shareable Report
Generate a self-contained HTML snapshot that preserves all results, notes, flags and discrepancies. Share via email or link — the recipient sees everything exactly as you left it, no files needed.
🕐 History
Comparison History & Audit Log
Auto-save every comparison to browser storage with timestamp and policy number. Reopen any past review, see what was flagged, what notes were added — full audit trail for compliance and E&O defence.
🏷 Smart
Form Description Normaliser
When "Sanctions Suspension Clause" and "Sanctions Limitation Clause" appear on the same policy, detect that they may be duplicates or conflicting and flag for UW review. Stop silently passing bad policies.
📋 Export
RISCO Email Draft Generator
After comparison, generate a ready-to-send email to the retail agent listing missing forms, discrepancies and required follow-up — formatted, professional, copy-paste ready in one click.
